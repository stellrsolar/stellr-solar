Why Stellr Solar

Our Core Commitment: Uncompromised Quality & Safety

1. Safety Built on CEC and OH&S Excellence

Every installation is treated as critical infrastructure. We design and install all systems to meet or exceed the Canadian Electrical Code (CEC), ensuring compliance with rules like Rapid Shutdown (Rule 64-218). Our teams follow strict OH&S practices, including daily Field Level Hazard Assessments and full WCB/WSIB compliance.

2. Products Engineered for Durability

We use only Tier-1 modules rated for a 5400 Pa snow load and optimized temperature coefficients. Whether your system uses String Inverters or Microinverters, each design is tailored for performance, safety, and long-term reliability.

3. Partnership That Lasts 30+ Years

We stay with you long after installation. From annual performance checks to thermal imaging diagnostics, our proactive maintenance ensures maximum lifetime output. All systems are protected under our 25-year product and 30-year performance warranties.
